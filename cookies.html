Privacy & cookies

How we handle your data

Hoinam Energy uses essential cookies to keep your account, cart, and checkout running securely.

Cookie use

We use session cookies to keep you signed in, hold cart items, and connect your checkout to Paystack. Preference cookies help remember the pages you last visited and your chosen options.

  • Essential: Login, cart, checkout, and installation forms.
  • Performance: Basic usage signals so we can improve ordering and booking.
  • Marketing: We do not run third-party marketing cookies on this site today.

Personal data

Your name, email, phone number, and delivery details are stored in our secure database so we can fulfill orders and coordinate installations. Payment data is processed by Paystack and never stored on our servers.

Manage your choice

You can clear cookies from your browser at any time. If you decline cookies, some features like login, cart, and checkout may not work as expected.

Review our privacy policy for details on how we protect your data.
