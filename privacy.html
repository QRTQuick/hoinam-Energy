Privacy policy

How Hoinam Energy protects your data

We only collect the information needed to deliver products, process payments, and schedule installations.

Information we collect

We collect contact details (name, email, phone), delivery and site addresses, and order details when you purchase products or request installation support.

  • Account data: Name, email, and phone number.
  • Order data: Products purchased, quantities, and delivery preferences.
  • Installation data: Site address, dates, and service notes.

Payments

All card payments are handled securely by Paystack. Hoinam Energy does not store your card details. We only keep the payment reference needed to confirm your order.

How we use your data

We use your data to deliver products, coordinate installation, provide support, and comply with legal requirements. We do not sell customer data to third parties.
